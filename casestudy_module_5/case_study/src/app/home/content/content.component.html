WELL COME TO FURUMA

Furama is a premier base for exploring one of Asia’s most exciting new destinations. Just a short drive from Danang lay four UNESCO-listed World Heritage Sites

BOAT TOUR – FISHING – SNORKELING

It takes about 20 minutes by a speed boat or 40 minutes by a fishing boat from Furama Resort’s beach to Son Tra peninsula also known as Monkey Mountain.

TAI CHI

While at the beach don’t miss out on our daily Tai Chi lesson or the numerous other activities that change daily throughout your visits such as yoga lessons badminton competitions.

CHILDREN’S ACTIVITIES

A private babysitter can also be arranged if requested. It should be reserved at least 24 hours in advance.
